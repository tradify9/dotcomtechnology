    Shipping Policy

    At DotcomTechnologies, we strive to deliver your orders swiftly and securely. Partnered with leading logistics providers, we ensure reliable delivery services across India.

    Order Processing

    Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

    Shipping Services

    Delivery Timeframe: Post-dispatch, orders are typically delivered within 4-8 business days, depending on your location. Remote areas may take longer due to logistical constraints.

    Shipping Charges: Shipping costs are determined based on order weight and delivery location. The final shipping fee will be shown at checkout before payment.

    Order Tracking

    Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. Track your shipment on our logistics partner's website using this number.

    Delivery Attempts

    Our logistics partner will attempt delivery up to three times. If delivery fails after these attempts, the package will be returned to our warehouse. Contact our support team to arrange re-delivery or resolve any issues.

    Damaged or Lost Packages

    If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please notify our customer support within 48 hours of the expected delivery date. We will coordinate with our logistics partner to address the issue promptly.

    International Shipping

    Currently, DotcomTechnologies ships only within India. For international inquiries, please contact our support team.
